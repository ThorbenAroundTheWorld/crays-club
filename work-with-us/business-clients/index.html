Beginning of a new era

‍The 2020s mark the beginning of a new era. Decentrelized lving and working will soon become mainstream. Innovative employers start providing their employees a global infrastructure. Companies that adapt quickly will have a competitive advantage in the employee marketplace, in addition to the obvious cost advantage, by offering employees the freedom and flexibility they demand and, most importantly, the responsibility and self-confidence that comes with it.

Millenials set the tone

Millennials & Gen Z who grew up in the midst of the digital age-are the first generation to see the world through the lens of the Internet, which gives them a shared global culture and a greater awareness of remote locations from a young age. They face a choice between being tied to a rigid employer and landlord in a single, expensive city or for independent living in multiple locations. Research shows that Millennials value memorable experiences over material possessions and also employer flexibility above all else.

Subculture to lifestyle

Over 25% of jobs worldwide will be remote work jobs in the near future. Home office work is the current status quo , but for most, only the beginning of a new era. Before the end of this decade, while some countries retreat into nationalism and isolationism, employees of international companies, will take a different path - that of decentralized work and global living. The signs are already clear, in a global subculture that is almost doubling every year and whose lifestyle is about to become mainstream.

FAQs

Answers to frequently asked questions

Do I need to set up my entire corporation or a specific team?

Either option works, ultimately you decide the final number of Memberships to purchase.

I work for a large corporation. Can you set up more than 100 members?

Yes, please schedule a meeting with our team to get a quote for 100+ members package.

Can I transfer the membership to another employee during the year?

Yes, if an employee has left the company, or for any other reason cannot use a membership, you can transfer the membership to another employee.

How am I billed for Business Memberships?

You will receive a Stripe invoice for the total amount; it will be billed monthly. The memberships automatically renew, if you don´t terminate them.

Can I increase or decrease the number of memberships?

Of course! If you gain new employees or new teams want to participate, please contact your Crays Account Manager to purchase new memberships. If you want to decrease the number of memberships you can terminate the memberships monthly.
