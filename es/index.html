Guest Journey

Un club flow desde la llegada hasta el afterwork.

The Crays Club is designed around the guest's real day: check in, find the right people, work privately, meet publicly, recharge, create content, enjoy the evening and return with the same identity in the next city.

01

Arrive & Belong

Walk into a familiar Crays setting, meet the local circle and feel at home before the hotel routine begins.

02

Work Without Friction

Use equipped desks, meeting rooms, phone boxes, private office space and easy check-in when the day needs focus.

03

Lounge, Eat & Shop

Move into the lounge for food, beverage, retail, convenience products, fresh items and relaxed social energy.

04

Create & Present

Record, pitch or host inside podcast rooms, video rooms, content creator spaces and cinema-style presentation rooms.

05

Recover & Stay

Use pool, spa, sauna, gym and health services, then extend the visit through short or long stay club living.

06

Match & Return

Your Crays guest profile can follow you from Palma de Mallorca to future clubs, venues, rooftops and beach destinations.

The Club Mix

Work, lounge, living and health in one guest-facing space.

The Palma de Mallorca concept is structured around four clear guest needs: The Co-Working, The Lounge, The Living and The Health. Each part works on its own, but the real value is the day moving naturally from one mode to the next.

Co-Working Equipped workplaces, meeting rooms, phone boxes and private work zones.
Lounge Self-service food, bar, cozy interiors, retail stations and fresh products.
Living Short and long stay rooms with modern in-room technology and city access.
Health Pool, spa, sauna, large gym and lifestyle health products.

Lifestyle Culture

Crays is where strangers become stories.

The club is not only a place to sit down. It is a real-world social layer for people who want more than passive feeds: hotel guests, city locals, remote workers, founders, creators, artists, music people, fashion people and lifestyle brands meeting in the same physical environment.

From private dinners and brand partner events to afterwork sessions and founder circles, Crays Clubs are built to make the guest experience feel local, social, useful and premium.
