Hybrid working models with CRAYS

Remote work isn’t always easy. Some days it’s hard. Having the right corporate structure combined with the right culture makes all the difference.

The Challenge

‘While remote is a good tool, people need people. We need to interact with one another. We need to see faces and exchange ideas, so we need to meet sometimes.’

The Solution

Here Crays comes into the game. CRAYS gives a solid structure in places where members don’t know the culture. Crays offers dedicated living areas and offices which allows to use spaces whenever and wherever it’s convenient.

Result

All members can use CRAYs living and office spaces when they need a place to live, a desk or a meeting space with privacy, whether they’re in their home city or thousands of miles away. And while offering this freedom saving a lot of money.
