The community layer

A Crays Club is built for the whole day, not for one isolated use case.

Community Life connects the practical and emotional sides of global living. A member should be able to arrive in a city, find a productive base, meet the right people, join a hosted moment and feel part of a local circle before the week is over.

Hosted culture
