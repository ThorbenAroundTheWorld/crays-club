Association Team

The team layer built to execute Crays.

The Crays Business Nomads Association is not presented here as a Fund team. It is the association governance and execution layer behind the ecosystem: people from finance, technology, hospitality, real estate, law, risk, events and Web3 coordinated through one operating structure.

Governance architecture

The association keeps decisions structured and execution moving.

The Articles page explains the logic: the association coordinates purpose, standards, member groups, project approvals and use of shared resources. Dedicated project, IP, fund or operating entities execute commercial activity where it belongs.

Partner governance

Entrepreneurs who operate their own Crays Businesses inside the ecosystem under the Crays brand as independent partners automatically receive a seat in the Extended Steering Committee and can be elected into the Steering Committee.

Execution workstreams

The team is organized around the things Crays must actually build.

The committee structure is only useful if it produces execution. The workstreams below translate the Steering Committee material and the Articles logic into the practical capabilities needed for Crays Clubs, technology, finance vehicles, partner projects and member governance.

Governance

Swiss association, Council logic, DAO setup, member groups, voting, committees and documented approvals.

Capital

Funding ecosystem, tokenized shares, RWA bridges, investor relations, financial modeling and asset-backed structures.

Real estate

Portfolio management, cost planning, construction overview, asset operations, living concepts and development discipline.

Hospitality

Crays Clubs, PMS, guest tech, in-venue hardware, events, awards, lounge operations and partner standards.

Technology

AI, blockchain, Web3, Nostr, Lightning, local mesh, data systems and product execution across real venues.

Risk and compliance

Project controlling, due diligence, legal, tax, securities, operational risk and governance guardrails.

How execution flows

From idea to project without losing governance.

01

Propose

A member, partner, venue, investor or builder brings a project, capital idea or operational need into the association process.

02

Structure

The General Secretariat clarifies role, entity path, legal route, finance logic, standards and required approvals.

03

Review

Steering and extended specialists review finance, legal, technology, real estate, hospitality, brand and risk implications.

04

Approve

The appropriate association body, member group, Council or project structure decides under the Articles and internal rules.

05

Execute

Executive teams, partners and local operating entities build under Crays standards while the association monitors alignment.
